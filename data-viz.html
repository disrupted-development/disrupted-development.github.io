Education

These interactive visualizations show how U.S. foreign assistance disruptions have affected education programs globally. Explore patterns of funding change by country and region across the FA Education sector.

About the Data

Data for this visualization were provided directly by the Foreign Assistance (FA) platform and reflect allocations related to U.S. development and humanitarian programs. Additional context and updates are drawn from the Career Pivot Blog (2025), which analyzes the Department of Defense (DoD) and U.S. State Department budget cuts affecting FA programs.

This dashboard focuses on the Education sector (FA Sector 113), which includes programs in basic, secondary, and higher education, teacher training, and workforce development. The dataset used here can be accessed for reference via this Google Sheet.

Source: U.S. Foreign Assistance Data (FA, 2025); Career Pivot Blog (2025); analysis by the Disrupted Development research team.

Health

This dashboard illustrates how changes to U.S. foreign assistance have influenced global health programs, including disease prevention, health systems strengthening, and maternal and child health.

About the Data

The data come from the same FA dataset, which provides detailed breakdowns of funding by sector and region. This visualization focuses on Health (FA Sector 122), encompassing disease control initiatives, nutrition, and health infrastructure investments worldwide.

The information reflects the consequences of 2025 DoD and State Department funding shifts, discussed in the Career Pivot Blog. Original FA data are organized by country, fiscal year, and sector classification and can be explored through the public Google Sheet.

Source: FA Data (2025); Career Pivot Blog (2025); analysis by the Disrupted Development research team.
