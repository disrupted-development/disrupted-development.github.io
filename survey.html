Policy Impact Survey

As part of the Disrupted Development documentation process, we are collecting survey responses from individuals and organizations that may have been affected by recent policy changes related to global development financing and program implementation.

Your perspective will help us better understand how these changes are being experienced in practice and will strengthen the evidence base for this research effort. We invite you to complete the survey if you are in a position to share relevant experiences or observations.
